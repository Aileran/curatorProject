Credits

This masterpiece before you is the culmination of Ian Tierney and Josh Schilling putting in maximum effort to produce a web app as a final project for their Web Programming class CS 416 at CCSU with Professor Albayram. No work had started until roughly a week before the due date when Josh, God bless the man, remarked to Ian that maybe, just maybe, this project deserved their consideration. Sensing the wisdom in this, Ian vehemently concurred, claiming they'd, "bang the sucker out no problem, and with enough time to spare for a day trip to Boston. Alas, day trips are out of the question what with Covid, but they did (debatably) finish the website in time.
